About Me

Graphic Designer turned Web Developer

Hey there! I'm Esmee, a creative mind with a passion for bringing digital ideas to life. I graduated from Nottingham Trent University in 2023 after studying Graphic Design and achieved a 2.1.

During my university years, I discovered a particular interest in crafting visually appealing websites and user interfaces. The process of bringing designs to life on the digital canvas ignited a new spark within me, leading me to embark on an exciting adventure into the realm of web development.

Now, I am enthusiastically honing my skills in web development, eager to transform my design concepts into functional and interactive websites and apps. I have more interested in the front-end of development as I am visual person but I am studying to become a full-stack developer which will allow me to understand all areas of development and my goal is to merge the realms of design and development seamlessly, creating digital spaces that not only look visually appealing but also function flawlessly.
